BEEF BOURGUIGNON

MODERNIST ADAPTATION
Yields 1200 g
Ref: MBB-092-V2
INGREDIENT QUANTITY SCALING PROCEDURE
Beef Short Ribs 800 g 100 %
01

Season ribs with salt. Sear in a high-heat pressure cooker until deep brown crust forms on all sides. Remove beef and set aside.
Red Wine (Burgundy) 400 g 50 %
02

Deglaze the pressure cooker with red wine, scraping all fond from the base. Reduce liquid by half to concentrate esters.
Beef Stock (Brown) 200 g 25 %
03

Return beef to the vessel. Add stock and aromatics. Seal and pressure cook at 1 bar (15 psi) for 90 minutes.
Aromatics (Mirepoix) 120 g 15 %
04

Depressurize rapidly. Strain sauce through a fine chinois. Emulsify with cold butter if necessary for gloss and viscosity.
Pearl Onions & Carrots 80 g 10 %
05

Glaze vegetables separately in 100°C steam. Combine with beef and reduced sauce immediately before service at 65°C.

Critical Equipment

  • Pressure Cooker (15 psi)
  • Chinois (Fine Mesh)

Core Temperature

Final serving temperature: 65°C. Sauce viscosity: 250 mPa·s at serving temp.

Yield Accuracy

Calculated evaporation loss: 8.4%. Adjust stock if deviation exceeds 2%.
